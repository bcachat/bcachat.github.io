Introducing the chat website
made exclusively for BCA students!

How's your social life going? The answer is probably not that well, right? We all say that a BCA student can only have two of the following things: good grades, extracurriculars, and a social life. As fellow BCA students, the Shaadi Girls know that the struggle is real. We have firsthand seen how all too often, we decide to eliminate the social life from our choices. But did you know that NOW you can keep your social life? That's right! The answer to all your social problems is here! Use BCA chat website! Brought to you by the Shaadi Girls, BCA Chat is a new, free website that will allow you to message fellow BCA alumni. You could message your friends, chat with new people, and if you are up for some fun you could even use our anonymous chat feature! Now you may be worried about your personal safety on this website. However, BCA chat is completely safe to use as ONLY BCA students can use it. In addition, most social media websites are blocked on the school's wifi. BCA Chat is there for other means of communication! Who knew that a social life was possible at a crazy school like BCA?

Efficiency

This website is very efficient, so the nerdy, dorky students of BCA can make friends with other BCA students. In addition it serves as an alternative from Facebook being blocked in school!

Make new friends!

BCA chat allows you to message your friends as well as make new friends (or not) by using our anonymous chatting feature!

Accessibility

This site is very accessiblle! It can be accessed on a desktop, laptop, or mobile device, so the nerds of BCA can chat or make more BCA dorky friends with ease!!!

Security

None of the information entered in the site will be shared with any third party members. You can trust this site with your personal information! However, be aware that you are responsible for everything that you say and do on this website, so be careful with your words.
