Introducing the chat website
made exclusively for BCA students!

How's your social life going? The answer is probably not that well, right? We all say that a BCA student can only have two of the following things: good grades, extracurriculars, and a social life. As fellow BCA students, we know that the struggle is real. We have firsthand seen how all too often, we decide to eliminate having a social life from our choices. But did you know that NOW you can keep your social life? That's right! The answer to all your social problems is here! Use BCA chat website! BCA Chat is a new, free website that will allow you to message fellow BCA alumni. You can message your friends, chat with new people, and you can even use our anonymous chat feature! Now you may be worried about your personal safety on this website. However, BCA chat is completely safe to use as ONLY BCA students can use it. So don't stress it! In addition, since most social media websites are blocked on the school's wifi, BCA Chat is now here for other means of communication! Who knew that a social life was possible at a crazy school like BCA?

Efficiency

For your convenience, this website is very efficient, so you can chat whenever you want. In addition, it serves as an alternative for other social media, which are mostly blocked by school wifi!

Make new friends!

BCA chat allows you to message your friends and make new friends with its anonymous chat feature!

Accessibility

This site is very accessible! It can be accessed on a desktop, laptop, or mobile device, so you can chat or make more BCA friends with ease, from any device!

Security

None of the information entered in the site will be shared with any third party members. You can trust this site with your personal information! However, be aware that you are responsible for everything that you say and do on this website, so be careful with your words.
